Academic Project · ESTACA Sep 2022 – May 2025

Volta — France's 1st Race-Homologated EV
1st Retrofit Peugeot 208 Cup

Co-founded and managed the Volta project — a team of 25 students tasked with converting a Peugeot 208 Cup into France's first FIA-homologated electric race car. I led the controls division: designed a full custom Simulink BMS, integrated a 3× CAN bus architecture, Motec VCU, and all FIA-spec safety systems. The car achieved official homologation and competed in 3 races in the Laval region.
